music

A huge part of my life is listening to and making music. I started playing violin when I was little, but these days I play jazz guitar. I love the musical conversations and spontaneous creativity that can happen during a jazz tune. This is just a little slice of the thousands of songs that inspire me.

Warning: Some of these video embeds don't work! Click the "Watch on YouTube" links if they're down for you.

third wind

Pat Metheny Group - Still Life (Talking)

My dad first played this jazz song for me when I was just starting to learn guitar. I was instantly hooked by this thing that sounded nothing like I've ever heard before. Pat Metheny is still my favorite guitarist, and I love to listen to the songs on this album over and over again.

strike up the band

count basie orchestra - basie swings standards (re-release)

This is another album I'm nostalgic for. I enjoy all of the big-band songs on this album, but I like this one particularly for its uptempo swing groove and exciting shout chorus.

years of therapy

gordon goodwin's big phat band - life in the bubble

It's too bad that the "big band" genre never really gets broken down beyond that, because the stuff that modern big bands play is like night and day when compared to the classic song above. The Big Phat Band has so many creative tunes that push the definition of what big band music sounds like.

crystal silence

Chick Corea / Arr. by Mike Tomaro

I played this slower song at my school before I even heard the original. I know it's a bit silly, but to be honest, I like this arrangment maybe more than the original! I'm just disappointed the best recording I found of this version is the score read-along preview, since it isn't on any music streaming platforms.

against the fall of night

sungazer - against the fall of night

As I've grown as a musician, I've realized how much I love jazz fusion specifically. Sungazer's music hits that math/prog feeling that no other band seems to capture quite the same way. Since this album released, I've been listening to the title track nonstop!
